Articles & Publications

The Journal welcomes research articles that make a notable contribution to the scientific and academic community. Upon submission, each paper will be promptly assigned a Unique Manuscript Number within 24 hours. We employ a double-blind review process, ensuring the anonymity of both reviewers and authors during the review period. The review process is typically concluded within 10 days of submission. Following acceptance, manuscripts are swiftly published online, and the corresponding author will receive the article proof by mail. Authors are advised to review the Instructions to Authors prior to submitting their manuscript.
