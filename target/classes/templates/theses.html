Featured Theses: APMR

We offer swift bi-monthly publication of various scholarly works, including research articles, case reports, review articles, correspondences, and short communications across all subject areas.

The Journal welcomes research articles that make a notable contribution to the scientific and academic community. Upon submission, each paper will be promptly assigned a Unique Manuscript Number within 24 hours.

We employ a double-blind review process, ensuring the anonymity of both reviewers and authors during the review period.

The review process is typically concluded within 10 days of submission.

Following acceptance, manuscripts are swiftly published online, and the corresponding author will receive the article proof by mail.

Authors are advised to review the Instructions to Authors prior to submitting their manuscript.

Why Publish with APMR

  • Rapid publication post-acceptance.
  • Each article is assigned a DOI (Digital Object Identifier System).
  • Track submitted articles online
  • Manuscript submission is entirely online.
  • Receive updates on your manuscript via email and SMS.
  • A blind peer-reviewed and open-access journal.
  • Articles are abstracted/indexed by major scientific indexing services.
  • Original articles can be downloaded (PDF) from www.journalsapmr.com.
